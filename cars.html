Cars & Boats

The first year the D&E Club started was also the first year of middle school for me. I've seen the growth of the club since the very first meeting day of the club to now. We went from building cars with hand pulled steering to cars with steering wheels and boats which could easily flip over to clear bottom boats which were much more stable. It has been an amazing experience keeping in touch with the club to both help out with teaching new students how to woodwork and continue to work on my own woodworking projects.

Since it was the first year the D&E Club started, we only made cars that year. Over the years, we created new designs for both the cars/boats and expanded upon what we had built in previous years.
