Wooden Couch

Estimated Hours: 25

This couch was the first piece of furniture I had made. I learned many different furniture woodworking techniques such as how to make a mortise and tenon joint. It took several sessions at the woodworking shop to finish because furniture requires you to be more precise. This couch was made with pieces of oak wood which had to be put into the planer and jointer to make flat planks of wood.
